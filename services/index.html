Clear answers before we start

Every strategy is customized. Here are quick answers to the most common questions about timelines, budgets, and reporting.

If you have a specific goal, I will map a plan that fits your budget and timeline.

How long does it take to see results from SEO?

SEO is a long-term strategy. Typically, you can start seeing improvements within 3-6 months, with more significant results appearing after 6-12 months. The timeline depends on your website's current state, competition, and keywords.

What's the minimum budget for Google Ads?

The minimum budget varies by industry. I recommend starting at $500/month to collect useful data, then scaling once we see consistent conversions.

Do you offer monthly reporting?

Yes. You receive a monthly performance report plus clear action items for the next cycle. Weekly updates are available for active campaigns.

Can you work with small businesses?

Absolutely. I work with startups and SMEs and adapt strategies to your budget while focusing on fast, measurable wins.

What makes your approach different?

I focus on measurable results, not vanity metrics. You get direct communication, transparent reporting, and strategies tied to your business goals.
