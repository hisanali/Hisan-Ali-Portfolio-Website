Cookie Policy

This page explains what cookies are and how they are used to improve your experience.

Last updated: January 26, 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember preferences and understand usage patterns.

How We Use Cookies

  • Analytics: to measure traffic and improve site content.
  • Performance: to understand how the site and tools like the speed test are used.
  • Preferences: to remember basic settings when applicable.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services (such as analytics providers) that set their own cookies. These providers process data according to their policies.

Managing Cookies

You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings. Note that some site functionality may not work as intended if cookies are disabled.

Updates

We may update this policy to reflect changes in how cookies are used. The latest version will always be available here.
